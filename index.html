FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know about working together.

How long does it take to build a website? +
Timeline depends on the package and complexity. A basic informational site typically takes 2-3 weeks from kickoff to launch. Small business sites take 3-4 weeks, and ecommerce sites take 4-6 weeks. Custom applications are quoted based on requirements.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch? +
Yes! I offer monthly maintenance packages that include updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and technical support. You can also reach out for one-off updates or changes as needed.
Can I update the website myself after it's built? +
Absolutely. For packages that include CMS integration, you'll be able to update content, add blog posts, and manage your site through an easy-to-use dashboard. I provide training and documentation to get you comfortable with it.
What if I need changes after the project is complete? +
Each package includes rounds of revisions during the project. After launch, small tweaks are typically included for the first 30 days. Larger changes or new features can be quoted as add-on work.
Do you work with clients outside of Philadelphia? +
Yes! While I'm based in the Philadelphia metro, I work with clients remotely across the US. Communication happens via email, video calls, and project management tools to keep everything smooth.
